1) Job Creation and New Opportunities:

While AI will replace some jobs, it will also create new opportunities for skilled workers, such as: AI maintenance, robotics engineering, and data analysis.

2) Enhancing Job and Education Performance:

AI will aid workers by handling other tasks, which will allow them to focus on other aspects of their jobs. Students will also benefit and learn using AI if they use correctly.
